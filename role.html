Role & Contribution

> >

As a forest guide at Gua Kelam, Encik Azim is responsible for guiding visitors safely throughout the cave while sharing information about the history, structure, and uniqueness of Gua Kelam. He ensures that tourists follow safety procedures and enjoy a comfortable experience during their visit.

Besides guiding visitors, Encik Azim also plays an important role in monitoring the condition of the cave and surrounding forest areas. He helps maintain the cleanliness and safety of the tourist attraction to preserve its natural beauty for future generations.

Encik Azim is also involved in exploring and identifying new cave tracks that can potentially become future eco-tourism attractions in Perlis. His experience and knowledge greatly contribute to the development of tourism activities related to cave exploration and nature tourism.

In addition, he educates visitors about the importance of protecting nature and preserving the environment. Through his role, he contributes significantly to promoting Gua Kelam as one of the most unique and attractive tourism destinations in Perlis.
